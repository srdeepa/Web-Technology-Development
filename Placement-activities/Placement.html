Placement

Placement office encourages students to gain a great deal of knowledge during their first year when being trained in various languages. Functional English & German language training is offered to students during their 1st year of engineering.

Our students are eager to extend their knowledge and gain greater insight into operational processes by fulfilling a practical placement, possibly at companies. In order to offer a Practical Placement, placement officer and the companies involve themselves in a lot of industry institute activities which are as follows:

  • Regular Students development programs are offered by domain specific core companies, in means of Technical workshops, seminars and quiz programs.
  • Regular Industry visits will be organized.
  • Special scholarships will be given out to students who are achievers.
  • Encouraging students to participate in Hackatons and Coding contests.
  • Coordinate with companies in organizing workshops on latest trends in the industries specific to core sectors.
  • Involving companies in syllabus formation and delivery of new courses to students.
  • During the final year of their B.E / M.Tech & MCA program, students work in and for companies as interns. In this way, they gain valuable practical experience while bringing their own added value of knowledge and expertise to the companies they are working for. Our students have a wealth of experience and knowledge; they are eager to learn and are able and willing to contribute to the company’s business.
